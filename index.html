Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Boreal Solution ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Soapstone mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Offline-First Design: Soapstone is built as a local, offline-first tool. By default, your calculation history and input parameters stay completely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

1. Information Collection and Use

While using our App, we may collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

On-Device Data Storage

All calculations, pipe weights, fitting variables, and history records are written directly to an embedded SQLite database running locally on your device. We do not have access to this local data.

User-Submitted Feedback

If you explicitly request a custom calculator or submit feature feedback via the in-app forms, this text is logged locally to help improve the App. These logs may be bundled and shared with us when you voluntarily submit diagnostic logs or beta test reports.

2. Analytics and Crash Reporting

To help us keep the App stable and improve the user experience, the App integrates with the following third-party services, which remain dormant/inactive unless telemetry configuration keys are supplied by us during the build:

  • Sentry: Used for real-time crash reporting and diagnostic error collection. If a crash occurs, Sentry collects anonymous device details and application logs to help us resolve the bug.
  • PostHog: Used for general product analytics. It registers when features are used (e.g., calculation buttons tapped) to let us know which tools are most useful. It does not track personal data.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect usage statistics and diagnostic info under the terms of their own policies:

  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. Because we do not store your data on external cloud databases, the security of your app data relies on the native security features of your mobile operating system.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not designed for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a new calculator?

At the bottom of the main calculator screen, tap the "Need a calculator we don't have? Tell us." button. Type in the calculations you need and send it. We check these requests regularly to decide what to build next.

Is my data backed up to the cloud?

Currently, all fitting variables and calculations are stored strictly offline in your local phone storage. If you delete the app or clear its data, your calculation history will be removed. Back up important cut sheets manually before deleting the app.

How do I report a calculation bug?

If a rolling offset or fitting angle calculation returns unexpected results, please take a screenshot of your inputs and send it to our support email below.
